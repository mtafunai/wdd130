HISTORY

Whitewater Rafting was born on 1978 as a way to bring amazing experience to intrepid people and share our love for the river rafting with everyone! We began offering guided trips on weekends, holidays as a small business, but quickly a lot of adventure-seekers come to us looking or exploring for new challenges. We have been growing ever since, and now we are the largest.

Nowadays we are in 9 areas in the country recogized internationally as a adventure companies.
